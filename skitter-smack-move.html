    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Skitter Smack (Japanese: はいよるいちげき Creeping Strike) is a damage-dealing Bug-type move introduced in Generation VIII. It is TM219 in Generation IX.

    Effect

    Skitter Smack inflicts damage and lowers the target's Special Attack stat by one stage.

    Generation VIII

    Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl

    Skitter Smack cannot be selected in a battle.
