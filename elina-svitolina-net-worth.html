    Elina Svitolina: A Ukrainian Tennis Star with a Net Worth of $12 Million

    Elina Svitolina, a professional tennis player from Odessa, Ukraine, has a net worth of $12 million. She turned professional in 2010 and has won over $15 million in prize money throughout her career. Her highest career ranking was at #3 in September 2017.

    Svitolina has won 13 WTA singles titles, including the 2018 WTA Finals, and defeated top players such as Serena Williams, Angelique Kerber, and Simona Halep. In 2019, she reached the semi-finals of both Wimbledon and the US Open and made it to the quarter-finals of the Australian Open in 2018 and 2019 as well as the French Open in 2015 and 2017.

    Her notable wins include the Dubai Tennis Championships, the Italian Open, and the Canadian Open in 2017 and the Italian Open again in 2018. She also won the Tour Finals in 2018 and reached the quarter-finals at the 2016 Olympic Games. Svitolina has been in a relationship with fellow tennis player Gael Monfils.
