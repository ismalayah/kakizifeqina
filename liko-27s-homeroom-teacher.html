    Liko's homeroom teacher is a minor recurring character in Pokémon Horizons: The Series. He is one of the teachers at Indigo Academy and teaches Liko and Ann.

    In the anime

    History

    Before the events of Pokémon Horizons: The Series, Liko's homeroom teacher was responsible for conducting Liko's interviews before joining Indigo Academy.

    Liko's homeroom teacher first appeared in a flashback in The Pendant That Starts It All (Part One). Later, he was responsible for delivering the first partner Pokémon of students in Liko's class, including her Sprigatito and Ann's Oshawott.

    Liko's homeroom teacher briefly appeared in The Door That Never Opens.

    In HZ035, he reappeared teaching a class about Pokémon and bonding with its Trainer. As part of the class, he assigned the class to choose a Trainer and their Pokémon to present in the next class. The next day, he was seen with the class watching Liko's dissertation on Friede and Captain Pikachu.

    In HZ040, he reappeared in a flashback to Liko's initial interview, where it was revealed that Manya was with the teacher during that interview and noticed enormous potential in the girl.
