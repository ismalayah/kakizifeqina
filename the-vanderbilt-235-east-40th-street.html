    Description of The Vanderbilt at 235 East 40th Street

    235 East 40th Street is a condo with 370 apartments, located in Turtle Bay/United Nations very close to the 4, 5, 6 and 7 subway lines.

    During the past two years, 27 apartments have sold: the most expensive was a two bedroom that sold for $1,250,000 (about $1,143 per sq. ft.), and the least expensive was a six plus bedroom that sold for $510,000 (about $1,275 per sq. ft.).

    Amenities at this post war building include doorman, pool, fitness center and full service garage.
